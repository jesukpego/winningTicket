Getting Started

Creating an account is quick and easy:

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
  2. Enter your email address and create a secure password
  3. Verify your email address by clicking the link we send you
  4. Complete your profile information
  5. Confirm you are 18 years or older

Once verified, you can start playing immediately!

You must be 18 years or older to participate in any lottery games on WINNING TICKET. We strictly enforce age verification to ensure compliance with gambling regulations and promote responsible gaming.

Important: If you are found to be under 18, your account will be suspended immediately and any winnings will be forfeited.

Quick Pick is our automatic number selection feature that randomly generates numbers for you. This is perfect if you:

  • Don't have favorite numbers
  • Want to save time
  • Believe in pure random chance

The numbers are generated using our certified random number generator, ensuring complete fairness.

Did you know? Over 70% of jackpot winners used Quick Pick!

Account & Security

To reset your password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page

  3. Check your email for the password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password
  5. Log in with your new password
Tip: For security, password reset links expire after 24 hours.
