FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I claim my prize?

Prizes under $600 are automatically credited to your account. For larger prizes, you'll need to complete a claim form and provide identification. We'll guide you through the process step by step.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes! We use 256-bit SSL encryption and follow strict data protection regulations. We never sell or share your personal information with third parties.

How long do withdrawals take?

Most withdrawals are processed within 24 hours. Bank transfers take 1-3 business days, while e-wallets are usually instant.

Can I play from outside the US?

Currently, we only accept players from within the United States where online lottery is legal. We're working to expand to more regions soon.

What is the minimum age to play?

You must be at least 18 years old to participate in any lottery games on our platform. Age verification is required during registration.

How are the numbers drawn?

We use certified random number generators (RNGs) that are regularly audited by independent testing agencies to ensure 100% fairness.
